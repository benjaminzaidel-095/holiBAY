Roles

Drop in your crew’s names (comma separated), then randomize roles. Each device can keep the same assignments or create their own chaos.

Role definitions
  • Lead Chicken · chooses next bar, cannot pick same direction twice in a row.
  • Barnkeeper · forces the group to leave each bar within ~20 minutes.
  • Oracle · whispers one fake prediction per bar about what will happen next.
  • Archivist · logs one ridiculous quote per bar to read at the end.
  • Saboteur · gently misdirects at least one person each transition.
  • Bard · summarizes the last bar in one sentence upon entering the next.
  • Timekeeper · actually watches the clock and keeps the crawl moving.
