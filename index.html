Game Overview

After dinner at China Live, Migs (the chicken) and Sara go ahead and post up at one bar on the map with the tab. Everyone else splits into hunter teams and starts searching.

Map

  • The hunt map is the bars listed in the Map tab (Li Po, Vesuvio, Maggie’s, Tupelo, Legacy, April Jean, Devil’s Acre, The Lucky Spot, The Saloon).
  • Migs may only sit or move between bars on this list.
  • Hunters may only search bars on this list.

Migs’ Rules

  • Picks a starting bar with Sara and sits there with the tab.
  • May move at most once every 30 minutes, except for one special Stealth Move where he can move early.
  • If hunters get photo evidence of Migs outside between bars, he must enter the nearest bar, buy a shot, and send a selfie.
  • Migs has a single “safe bar” agreed in advance. If he sits there 45 minutes without being found, he effectively “escapes” once.
  • Every 30-ish minutes, Migs sends either a true or false hint. He only gets a limited number of lies.

Hunters’ Rules

  • Pick a bar, walk in, check if Migs is there.
  • If Migs is not there: the team must buy and finish a drink; that bar is “burned” for them unless they have a clear reason he moved back.
  • Burned bars should be tracked in the Map tab. Try not to re-check unless you are sure he moved.
  • If you catch Migs outside between bars, you trigger his shot-selfie penalty.

Bingo as Meta-Game

  • Bingo squares represent meaningful states: bars burned, bad hints, catches, escapes, and endgame chaos.
  • One square per bar visit max for your team; no farming multiple squares at once.
  • Use Bingo to record how stupid or brilliant the night gets; the hunt (finding Migs) is still the main win condition.

Scoring (Optional)

  • Hunters: points for finding Migs first and for each Bingo line.
  • Migs: points for each 30 minutes survived, each misleading hint that sends people wrong, and any safe-bar escape window.
  • Or skip formal scoring and just use the leaderboard tab to log who actually called Bingo.
